Who We Are

Cipher's Nexlify is a forward-thinking global sourcing company dedicated to helping businesses discover, develop, and deliver innovative products. With years of experience and a global network, we bring unmatched value, speed, and quality to the supply chain. Think of Cipher Nexlify Global Sourcing as an extension of your purchasing department – We’ve been at the forefront of international global sourcing ever since the company was established in 1986.
