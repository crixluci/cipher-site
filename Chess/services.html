OUR SERVICES

Your trusted partner in global product sourcing and supply chain excellence.

Cipher prides itself on providing excellent levels of customer service and in particular we place great importance on regular communication with our customers at all stages of the project.

There is no direct cost for our service; our income is included in the part price so therefore giving you complete best value.

With Cipher you can be assured of full technical and commercial support throughout the project life span. All deliveries are arranged in line with your purchase orders or delivery schedules. All invoicing is done from our office in the UK.

It is our responsibility to manage the project from initial sample approval to volume production. We deal directly with the manufacturer and have a direct quality assurance and commercial contract with them.

We can provide warehousing and stock management if required.

Our extensive network of skilled designers and factories spanning various product categories empowers us to deliver design solutions that accelerate your design development process, moving swiftly towards mass production.

Our global team stands ready to offer unparalleled support across the globe. Dedicated to your success, we provide comprehensive solutions to guide you seamlessly through your supply chain journey, ensuring excellence at every turn
